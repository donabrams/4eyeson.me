Who We Are

All schools use data to tell a student's performance story in a meaningful, action-oriented way that communicates information in a format empowering families, schools, and students to work together.

How We Can Help

Schools are empowered to engage families meaningfully in data-driven practices through clear, visual representations of student performance sparking effective conversations.

For Parents

As a nation, K12 public schools are moving towards full adoption of the common core standards. Schools need better, more efficient ways to communicate this information about common standards in order to regularly and effectively motivate and empower families and students. Having clear, simple visual representations of data allows parents, teachers, and students to collaboratively look at a student's performance story, sparking meaningful conversations with actionable items that help propel the child forward.
